Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through a reality show of the same name in 2017. They debuted as a nine-member group in 2018 but currently consist of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N

It was a male idol debut project with the concept of "trainees versus JYP", the goal of which being the survival of all nine members to debut as a team.
